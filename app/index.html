Methodology

Population responses are cleaned (latest per user×question), times normalized to minutes, and an objective threshold f(x) is fitted as a monotone curve (isotonic regression over binned medians). The fitted curve is shipped as fitted_threshold.json and evaluated via linear interpolation.

Source & analysis: github.com/rastsislaux/poputi
